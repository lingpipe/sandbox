Classes for manipulating data from the NCBI MEDLINE Citation Index. MEDLINE is a collection of 13 million plus citations into the bio-medical literature maintained by the United States National Library of Medicine (NLM). It contains richly structured data including authors, affiliations, titles, abstracts, grants, medical subject headings (MeSH), etc.

This package includes tools to:

  • Download the medline distribution files from NLM: DownloadMedline
  • Build a searchable Lucene index over these entries: IndexMedline
  • Searcher the index: MedlineSearcher
  • Utilities to inspect raw data file: SplitMedlineXml
